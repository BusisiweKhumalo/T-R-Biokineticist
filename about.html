BIOKINETICS

Ruth Ramabu

Ruth Ramabu is a dedicated and compassionate professional with a rich background in biokinetics. She began her academic journey at the Tshwane University of Technology, where she earned her Diploma and B-Tech degree. Ruth furthered her studies at WITS, completing an Honours degree in Health Science with a specialization in Biokinetics. She is proudly registered with the Health Professional Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and the Biokineticist Association of South Africa (BASA). Ruth's extensive experience includes working with TUT High Performance Basketball and Netball, the Midrand Back and Neck Centre, and the Physio and Wellness Centre. Additionally, she contributes her expertise as a health specialist to the Olieven Development Centre (ODA), a nonprofit organization. Ruth's passion for biokinetics stems from her lifelong love of sports and exercise. Her journey was shaped by her personal experiences with sports-related injuries and rehabilitation, which ignited her desire to help others. She firmly believes that exercise is medicine and is dedicated to educating people about its benefits, particularly for the elderly, those with chronic conditions, and special populations. Ruth's personal experiences with injury and ongoing fitness maintenance give her a unique perspective on pain management and rehabilitation. Her empathetic approach ensures that she understands her clients' challenges and helps them achieve long-term health and wellness. As Ruth likes to say, "More pain, no gain. Less pain, more gain."
Re-educating ourselves about progressive learning leads to lasting health and wellness.

Teboho Chesane

Teboho Chesane is a seasoned biokineticist with a robust educational background and a passion for improving lives through exercise. He earned his Diploma in Sport Science and Human Movement Science & Recreation from North West University, followed by a B-Tech degree in Biokinetics from Tshwane University in 2016. Teboho is registered with the Health Professional Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and the Biokineticist Association of South Africa (BASA). In 2017, he completed his internship at the BMW Plant in Rosslyn, where he gained invaluable experience. With 7 years of biokinetics practice under his belt, Teboho has worked with several reputable institutions, including the Midrand Back and Neck Center, Midrand Physio Wellness, and TUT High Performance teams for basketball, soccer, and rugby. Teboho is deeply passionate about orthopedic rehabilitation, sport conditioning, and high-performance training. He firmly believes in the power of exercise as a natural remedy, famously saying, "Exercise is a drug without side effects."
His dedication to his field and his clients' well-being is evident in his commitment to providing exceptional care and guidance..
