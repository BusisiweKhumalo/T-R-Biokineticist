SERVICES

Exercise Prescription

Biokineticists asses individuals and prescribe specific exercise programes tailored to their needs. These programes may aim to improve cardio vascular fitness , strength , flexibility , balance , or overall functional capacity.

Rehabilitation

Biokineticists play a crucial role in rehabilitation following injury , surgury, or medical conditions such as stroke or heart disease. They design and supervise exercise programs to restore functions, mobility, and strength , helping individuals to recover and regain independence.

Chronic Disease Management

Biokineticists work with individuals who have chronic health conditions such as diabetes, hypertension ,obesity , or osteorthritis . They develop exercise programs to manage symptoms , improve health outcomes , and enhence quality of life.

Sports Perfomance Enhancement

Biokineticists Assist athletes in improving their perfomance through specialized training programs. They focus on strength and conditioning , injury prevention , biomechanics and sport-specific training to optimize athletic Perfomance.

Preventive Health Service

Biokineticists promote health and wellness through preventive strategies . They provide education and physical activity , lifestyle modification , and injury Prevention to reduce their risk of chronic diseases and enhence overall well-being.

Functional Movement Screening

Biokineticists conduct assesments to evaluate movement patterns ,identify muscular imbalances , and assess functional limitations. This information helps in designing targeted exercise interventions to improvement efficiency and reduce injury risk.

Ergonomics and Workplace Health

Biokineticists may offer services related to ergonomics and workplace health.They assess Workplace environments , identify ergonomics factors , and provide recommendations to optimize work stations and prevent musculoskeletal injuries.

Pain Management

Biokineticists work with individuals experiencing pain , such as back pain or joint pain , by implementing exercise-based interventions to alleviate symptoms, improve mobility and enhence function.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

incollaboration with other health care professionals, biokineticists provide exercise-based rehabilitation programs for individuals recovering from heart conditions or cardiac procedures. These programs focus on improving cardio overscular health, strength and overallfitness.

Injury Prevention and Rehabilitation

Biokineticists asses movement patterns ,biomachanics , and muscular imbalances to identify and address factors contributing to injury risk . They develop personalized exercise programes to prevent injuriesand facilitate rehabilitation following musculoskeletal injuries.
